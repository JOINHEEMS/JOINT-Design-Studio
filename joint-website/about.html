About Join.T Design Studio

Modern architecture and design rooted in craft, clarity, and connection.

Our Approach

"Join.T" reflects our belief in the beauty of precision and the power of connections. We create refined, thoughtful spaces where architecture, construction intelligence, and lived experience intersect. With a focus on clarity and craft, we design environments that serve and endure. For us, design is about finding the right balance—harmonizing form and function, concept and constructibility.

  • Design is both conceptual and crafted—an idea executed with precision.
  • Every project sits at the intersection of architecture, construction, and human experience.
  • Thoughtful details matter as much as bold forms.

Tyheem Joiner

Founder + Principal Designer

Tyheem Joiner is an architectural designer with over 10 years of experience creating modern residential and small-scale commercial projects. With a background in BIM, detailing, and project coordination, Tyheem focuses on delivering design that is both refined and build-aware.
